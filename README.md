# APEx-Curriculum-
AI Safety Pathway Exploration Program (APEx) — A 4-week beginner fellowship curriculum by AI Safety Hub Nigeria. Covers AI foundations, safety pathways, career planning, and next steps. Built for African fellows. Free to use and adapt.
## What This Is

APEx is a beginner-level fellowship curriculum that takes participants from 
zero background in AI safety to a completed Personal Pathway Plan and a 
shortlist of concrete next opportunities.

It is written in plain language, grounded in verified sources, and designed 
for fellows who may be encountering AI safety for the first time — 
regardless of technical background.

---

## Curriculum Structure

| Week | Title | Focus |
|------|-------|-------|
| Week 0 | Foundations | What is AI, its history, benefits, risks, and an introduction to AI safety |
| Week 1 | What Is AI Safety Really? | The AI safety landscape, African governance gaps, and foundational readings |
| Week 2 | The Different Pathways | Technical, governance, policy, field-building, communications, and industry |
| Week 3 | From Exploration to Action | Career framework, skill audit, and the Personal Pathway Plan template |
| Week 4 | Presentation and Next Steps | Plan presentations, mentorship matching, opportunities, and staying in the field |

---

## Who It's For

- **Fellows** joining the APEx programme
- **Facilitators** running AI safety introductory programmes in Africa
- **Educators** building AI safety curricula for non-Western contexts
- **Individuals** exploring AI safety independently and wanting a structured starting point

No technical background is required. The curriculum is designed to be 
accessible to anyone with curiosity and commitment.

---

## What's Included

Each week contains:
- Plain-language reading guides for all assigned resources
- Key concepts explained with analogies and examples
- Nigeria- and Africa-specific context and discussion prompts
- Verified sources with direct links
- Fillable templates and self-assessment tools (Week 3 and 4)
- Session agendas and peer feedback structures

---

## The Personal Pathway Plan

The centrepiece of the curriculum is the **Personal Pathway Plan (PPP)** — 
an 8-section structured template fellows complete in Week 3 and present in 
Week 4. It covers:

- Skill audit and knowledge self-rating
- Pathway fit and comparative advantage assessment  
- Plan A, Plan B, and Plan Z
- A 10-row 90-day action plan
- Career capital goals across 5 dimensions
- Accountability structure and review dates

---

## Opportunities Covered

The curriculum maps real, accessible AI safety opportunities tiered by 
readiness level — from hackathons accessible today (Apart Research, Successif and EA advising) to structured courses (BlueDot, SPAR) to advanced fellowships (MATS, GovAI).
